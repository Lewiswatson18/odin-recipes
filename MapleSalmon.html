Maple Salmon

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound salmon

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Stir together maple syrup, soy sauce, garlic, garlic salt, and pepper in a small bowl.

  2. Step 2

    3. Cut salmon into 4 equal fillets, place in a shallow glass baking dish, and coat with maple syrup mixture. Cover the dish and marinate salmon in the refrigerator for 30 minutes, turning once halfway.

  3. Step 3

    4. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

  4. Step 4

    5. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake salmon uncovered until flesh easily flakes with a fork, about 20 minutes.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 265 calories; protein 23.2g; carbohydrates 14.1g; fat 12.4g; cholesterol 67mg; sodium 633.1mg.
